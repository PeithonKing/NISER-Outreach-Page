About Us

   Science reigns supreme from the cradle to the grave of our life. To cater scientific thoughts to all and sundry is our goal; promoting research works is our motto; and to make the lives more comfortable is our relentless struggle. Our mission is to boost the reasearchants at every stage.

Our vision

   We believe that an exposure at the appropriate time in one's career can ignite proper minds in learning and their scientific pursuit develop rightly. Keeping in mind we invite teachers and students to have a glimpse of the research ambience at NISER and arrange for an exposure with a view to the concept of 'Learning by doing'. NISER outreach activities stretches its hands by providing a single window policy for the teeagers and graduates through both virtual and online mode. Seminal Activities, Workshop Programs, Webinars on different disciplines take place almost daily, even in Sundays.

   Restricted Open Day programs create immense opportunities for the aspirants as they get ample scope for exposure. NISER Open Day Programme is introduced to invite people from various institutions can visit NISER in order to have a glimpse of the scientific exposure along with the learning experience. NISER Educational trips involve schools and college visits in different departments. Face to face interaction with the Educational officers, may unearth new ways and possibilities for the enhancement of knowledge. NISER hosts the Odisha chapter of the Vigyan Pratibha project in collaboration with The Institute of Physics, Bhubaneswar and Homi Bhabha Centre For Science Education, Mumbai; and conducts teachers' training workshops as and whenever required.
